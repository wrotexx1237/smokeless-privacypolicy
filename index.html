Privacy Policy for SmokeLess

Last updated: March 2026

SmokeLess is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the SmokeLess mobile application handles information.

Information We Collect

The SmokeLess app does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or location.

The app may store basic user preferences locally on your device such as:

  • Number of cigarettes per day
  • Smoking goals
  • App settings

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Data Retention

All data used by the SmokeLess app is stored locally on the user's device. The data remains on the device until the user deletes the app or clears the app data.

Third-Party Services

The app may use standard Android system services but does not share personal user data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The SmokeLess app is not directed at children under 13 years old.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
