Privacy Policy for SmokeLess Pro Last Updated: March 14, 2026 Welcome to SmokeLess Pro. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using SmokeLess Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. 1. Information We Collect We collect various types of information for different purposes to provide and improve our service to you. A. Personal Data While using our application (especially during the "Onboarding" process), we may ask you for data related to your smoking habits: Smoking Habits: How many cigarettes you smoke per day, pack price, and the number of cigarettes per pack. Goals: Your reduction plan (Gentle, Standard, Aggressive) and wake/sleep times. Logs: The time when you record smoking a cigarette. B. Usage and Device Data We collect technical information automatically: Device Information: Brand, model, and operating system version. IP Address: Used for basic user identification and security. Platform: Whether you are using iOS or Android. C. Location (Optional) The app may request access to your location (GPS) to provide contextual data or regional statistics. This information is only collected with your explicit consent. D. Media and Camera (Optional) In the "Why I Quit" section, you can upload photos or use the camera to save motivational visuals. These photos are stored locally or in your secure storage. E. Financial Data (Donations) If you choose to make a donation via Stripe, we do not store your credit card details. All transactions are securely processed by Stripe. We only receive payment confirmation and a transaction ID. 2. How We Use Your Data We use the collected data to: Generate personalized cigarette reduction plans. Calculate your financial savings and health benefits. Send reminder and motivational notifications. Analyze app performance and fix bugs. Manage donation transactions. 3. Third-Party Services SmokeLess Pro integrates services from trusted partners for proper functionality: Stripe: For payment/donation processing. Google AdMob: For displaying ads (we collect the advertising ID to show relevant ads). Expo/Firebase: For managing push notifications. Postgres (Database): Our data is stored on secure servers to enable you to access your account from different devices. 4. Data Storage and Security Your security is our priority. Local Storage: Basic data is stored on your device using secure technologies (SecureStore). Transmission: All communication with our servers is done via the encrypted HTTPS protocol. Protection: We use industrial safeguards to prevent unauthorized access. 5. Your Rights (GDPR/Privacy) You have the right to: Request a copy of the data we have about you. Request correction of inaccurate data. Request full deletion of your data (via the "Data Management" section in Settings). Withdraw consent for notifications or location access at any time. 6. Children's Privacy Our service does not address anyone under the age of 18 (or the legal smoking age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. 7.
